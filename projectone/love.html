LOVE, MARRIAGE, AND LIFE PARTNERS

love? too difficult... there is no answer. It's a choice. You make a decision depending on what you value most. Whether it’s a feeling, passion,responsibility, personality, appearance… But in love, not everything is decided by one choice. Love is a process. Getting to know each other little by little- by experiencing happiness,joy, disappointment, and suffering together. You could say that’s also true about the process of life.I think that what keeps that process of loving is a sense of protection and responsibility. So in marriage if the most important thing is love, then protecting that love is the responsibility. When it comes to marriage and the criteria for choosing a spouse, the first and most important would be a person you love. The second would be recognizing a kind and good person. A good person is someone I wanted you to grow up to be, someone who is physically and mentally healthy, has a kind heart, and someone who has a passion. The third and final criteria would be finding a responsible person.
